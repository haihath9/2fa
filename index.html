What is TOTP and 2FA?

Two-factor authentication (2FA) is an additional layer of security used to protect online accounts. TOTP (Time-based One-Time Password) generates temporary verification codes that expire after a short period of time.

Services like Google, Facebook, Binance, GitHub, Discord, and many others use TOTP authentication apps to help secure user accounts against unauthorized access.
